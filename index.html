About me

I am a PhD Student at ENS Paris, CREST-ENSAE and CNRS, where I am working under the supervision of Gabriel Peyré and Marco Cuturi. My field of research is machine learning, with a special emphasis on optimization and optimal transport.

Before my PhD, I also had the chance to be supervised by Robert M. Gower and Charles Dossal for two internships revolving around convex and stochastic optimization during my masters studies.

I hold an MsC in management from ESSEC Business School and a Master in Applied Mathematics from ENSAE and Ecole Polytechnique.
