About me

I am a PhD Student at ENS Paris, CREST-ENSAE and CNRS, where I am working under the supervision of Gabriel Peyré and Marco Cuturi. My field of research is machine learning, where I focus on optimization and optimal transport, and like to develop methods that scale for multiomics. I also hold a Master in Applied Mathematics from ENSAE and Ecole Polytechnique. In a past life, I received an MSc in management from ESSEC.

Outside of my research, I like to write about Machine Learning and was fortunate to co-author the State of AI Report 2021, 2022, and 2023 with Nathan Benaich and Ian Hogarth. The State of AI Report analyses the most interesting developments in AI from four perspectives: research, talent, industry and politics.
