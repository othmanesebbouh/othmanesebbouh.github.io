About me

I am a PhD Student at ENS Paris, CREST-ENSAE and CNRS, where I am working under the supervision of Gabriel Peyré and Marco Cuturi.

Before, I was a Masters student at ENSAE and ESSEC Business School. I also had the chance to be supervised by Robert M. Gower and Charles Dossal for two internships revolving around convex and stochastic optimization during my masters studies.

My topics of research are broadly optimization, optimal transport, and their application to machine learning.
